Welcome to Bertie’s Theme Park! Located near Liverpool, Bertie’s is fun for the whole family. Whether you want chilled out, family friendly friends or you’re an adrenaline junkie, this is the place for you. We have a varied list of rides ranging from family rides, such as Bamboo Boy to thrill rides, like The Giggler. If you decide you want a break from rides, take a seat in one of our many restaurants, located all throughout the park, and have a bite to eat. We can guarantee there’s something for everyone. If you have further questions that cannot be answered after looking through the app, please feel free to look on our FAQ section or visit Guest Services onsite. We hope you have an amazing, fun-filled day at Bertie’s!
